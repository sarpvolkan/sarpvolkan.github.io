IDL-based Lagrangian particle-tracking algorithm is adapted for the automated detection and lifetime tracking of umbral dots, enabling one of the largest statistical analyses of Goode Solar Telescope (GST/BBSO) observations reported at the time (Solar Physics, 2018). By distinguishing true trajectory path length from net displacement, we demonstrated that mean umbral-dot velocities are nearly twice previously reported values—revealing a characteristic nonlinear wandering motion rather than simple radial inward propagation.
